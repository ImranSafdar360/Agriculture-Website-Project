Our Introduction

Agriculture & Organic
Product Farm

Our farm is a wholly owned indigenous Ghanaian company focusing on improving the crop production of Africa by providing affordable fertilizers to farmers, establishing commercial sized farms to produce crops such as cassava, millet, sorghum, maize, soya, etc. to bridge the gaps in the local supply as well as conduct soil tests on farms so as to accurately determine the fertilizer needs specific to each farm.
